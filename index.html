For 7+ years, AppleBox has been the production partner behind some of the most demanding shoots in Saudi Arabia. We shape imagination into cinematic experiences.

We go beyond managing projects. We build experiences. Every shoot, every campaign, every story we deliver blends creativity, precision, and innovation. From pre-production planning to on-set execution, our expert team handles every detail so you can focus on your vision.

Our Story
"We didn't set out to be a production company. We set out to be the reason productions don't fail."

It started with a single production van and a belief that Saudi Arabia deserved world-class production infrastructure. From those early shoots, AppleBox grew year after year, adding vehicles, crew, capabilities, and clients who trusted us to deliver.

Today we operate one of the largest dedicated production fleets in Saudi Arabia. We support international and regional productions across the Kingdom, providing everything from pre-production and crew coordination to fleet deployment and on-set catering. Our team of line producers, production managers, coordinators, drivers, and on-set specialists handles the full production workflow under one roof.

As Saudi Arabia's creative economy grows under Vision 2030, AppleBox is not catching up. We were already here, building the infrastructure that makes great productions possible.
